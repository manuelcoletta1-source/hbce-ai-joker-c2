```txt
Identity verified first.
Choice separated after.
Vote anonymized.
Process auditable.
```

```txt
IPR may support identity verification and participation rights.
```

Any production use in electoral, public-sector or regulated civic infrastructure requires:

```txt
legal review;
constitutional review;
privacy review;
security review;
independent audit;
institutional authorization;
public transparency;
human oversight;
```

```txt
auditability;
traceability;
operational identity;
runtime governance;
technical proof receipts;
verifiable decision support;
future institutional review.
```

Official certification, legal effect or public-sector adoption require external validation, legal review and institutional approval.
