## 3. Central Thesis

The central thesis of HBCE ECOSISTEMA AI is:

```txt
The problem is not only to have more powerful AI systems.
The problem is to govern AI inside identifiable, traceable, auditable and responsible operational processes.
```

In Italian:

```txt
Il problema non è soltanto avere AI più potenti.
Il problema è governare l’AI dentro processi identificabili, tracciabili, verificabili, auditabili e responsabili.
```

The collection therefore shifts the focus from AI output to AI process governance.

A generic AI model can generate an answer.

HBCE ECOSISTEMA AI asks whether the process that generated, used, reviewed, stored, traced or acted on that answer is governable.
