8. Core Analytical Sequence

APOKALYPSIS follows a threshold sequence.

Decadence -> Exposure -> Cognitive Dislocation -> Cost -> Recognition -> Cognitive Rupture -> Paradogma Alieno

Decadence

Decadence is not immediate collapse.

It is the condition in which a system continues to function while losing foundation.

Exposure

Exposure is the moment in which the loss of foundation becomes visible.

The system still operates, but the gap between operation and legitimacy becomes increasingly difficult to hide.

Cognitive Dislocation

Cognitive dislocation occurs when consciousness continues to live inside a system whose categories no longer explain the real conditions it produces.

Cost

Cost is the burden produced by the persistence of the system after foundation loss.

It may appear as social fragmentation, institutional exhaustion, cultural instability, psychological pressure or political contradiction.

Recognition

Recognition is the point where consciousness begins to see the dislocation.

It no longer confuses system continuity with system health.

Cognitive Rupture

Cognitive rupture occurs when consciousness can no longer be fully reintegrated into the old explanatory structure.

Paradogma Alieno

The Paradogma Alieno is the configuration that emerges when the old paradigm can no longer contain what reality has produced.

13. APOKALYPSIS Risk Categories

APOKALYPSIS work is usually low-risk when it remains analytical, theoretical and editorial.

Risk increases when the output names current actors, makes factual claims, discusses political conflict, addresses groups, or could be read as operational instruction.

Risk Category	Description

Current factual claim risk	Current events are used without verification
Political targeting risk	Analysis becomes directed against a person or group
Incitement risk	Text encourages unlawful action
Dehumanization risk	Language treats people or groups as less than human
Destabilization risk	Analysis becomes operational planning for disruption
Public communication risk	Threshold language is misunderstood as instruction
Coercive misuse risk	Theoretical framing is used to justify control or harm
Editorial continuity risk	Volume structure loses the sequence of decay, exposure and rupture


Default handling:

Risk Level	Handling

LOW	ALLOW
MEDIUM	AUDIT
HIGH	ESCALATE or DEGRADE
CRITICAL	ESCALATE or BLOCK
PROHIBITED	BLOCK
