HBCE Application Layer
AI JOKER-C2
Identity-bound operational AI application designed to operate within the HBCE infrastructure. AI JOKER-C2 connects persistent identity (IPR), governance validation, event registration and verification pipelines.

Operational AI Interface

AI JOKER-C2 provides a conversational and operational interface designed to execute requests under identity-bound governance.

IPR Identity Layer

Every runtime instance can be associated with an Identity Primary Record, allowing deterministic attribution of digital actions.

Evidence Pipeline

Operations may produce verifiable evidence traces, supporting append-only registries and external verification.

HBCE Infrastructure

AI JOKER-C2 operates as part of the HBCE ecosystem developed by HERMETICUM B.C.E. S.r.l.
