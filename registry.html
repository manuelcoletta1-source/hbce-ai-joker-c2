Federation posture

The registry is not limited to local node introspection. It is designed to support a federated topology in which node identity, continuity status, and verification surfaces can be exposed across a wider Matrix Europa network.

In this model, Torino acts as the first real node, while the registry structure remains compatible with multi-node growth.

Conclusion

The registry surface of AI JOKER-C2 exposes more than topology. It exposes the operational identity structure of the node, including primary roots, continuity state, verification posture, and the first derived identity branch generated through the Biocybernetic Derivation Layer.
